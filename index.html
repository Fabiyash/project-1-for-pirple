Singing in the Rain 😀

  • Authors:Mala Kumar, Manisha Chaudhry
  • Kaka lives in a dry land. He is a singer. Kaka goes to other villages. He sings at parties. One day, Kaka goes to a new village.He walks. He sings. His song is: Come to me, clouds!

  • Kaka hears a sound! Dup, Dup, Dup! What makes this beat? It is Dinoo the dinosaur! Kaka is glad to see Dinoo.Dinoo likes the song too! Kaka and Dinoo sing and dance. Come to me, clouds! Give me your rain!The clouds hear the song. The clouds come in. Kaka and Dinoo are glad. Funny clouds!
    • The rain comes down. They share an umbrella. They go on to the village. And they sing: Thank you for the rain!

    Did you ever see a dinosaur? Do you think it could sing? What do you like to do in the rain?
